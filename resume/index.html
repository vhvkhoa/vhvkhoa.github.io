Research Areas

My research interests lie at the junction of deep learning and computer vision. Some representatives of the projects that I have been working and had publications on are image captioning, video dense captioning, and temporal action proposals.

I am also developing my research in 3D vision, in which I specifically work on 3D object reconstructions and tracking.
